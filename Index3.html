ABOUT HOMOEOPATHY

Homoeopathy originated from the experiments of Samuel Hahnemann a Physician who at the turn of the eighteen Century was dissatisfied with Speculative methods of Contemporary medicine and proceeded to “Prove” i.e. experimentally test, about hundred medicines upon him and other health people. He held that the evaluation of a drug effects should be based on the reaction of an average human organism. Since his time the proving have been extended to thousands from every kingdom and the symptoms carefully recorded and tabulated in their particular details. In these experiments human “provers” people of averages health, take repeated doses of especially prepared form of substances until symptoms appears ; symptoms which can be observed with a particular regularity in the majority of the “prover” are considered to express the pathogenic effects of this particular drug. When in turn, the symptom Complex of any Spontaneous illness is compared with artificial symptom complexes produces by drug, there often will be found an extraordinary close resemblance between the disease picture and the effects of one the drug on healthy persons. The drugs whose symptomotogies most clearly resemble the Symptom-complex of the sick person have been found clinically to be the most successful drug for the treatment of the condition.
